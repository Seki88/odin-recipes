Chicken Rogan Josh

Traditional Indian dish made with juicy chicken thighs and a wide variety of delicious spices.

Ingredients:

  • 1 kg chicken thighs
  • salt
  • black pepper
  • 1/2 can (200 g) chopped tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • 1 big onion
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp ground paprika
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 2 tsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 4 cloves
  • 6 cardamom pods
  • coriander
  • 250 ml chicken stock
  • 6 tbsp yoghurt

Steps

  1. Cut the chicken thighs into small pieces and season with salt and pepper
  2. Melt the butter in the pan and start cooking the chicken thighs. Cook until golden then take the meat out of the pan but leave the pan on the stove
  3. Meanwhile make the spices paste. Take a small food processor and in it put the onion, garlic cloves, tomato paste, olive oil, ground cumin, ground coriander, ground paprika, chili powder, ground turmeric and mix everything together to get the paste
  4. Put the cinnamon stick, bay leaves, cloves and cardamom pods in the pan and roast them for a few minutes for them to drop the flavours in the fat
  5. Add chopped tomatoes, spices paste, chicken thighs and chicken stock in the pan and simmer for a while. Then add the yoghurt 1 tablespoon at a time and mix it in. Simmer some more and then turn of the stove and mix in 1 teaspoon of garam masala at the end
